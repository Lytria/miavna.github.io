Miavna

Miavna is an emotional and deeply atmospheric narrative-driven puzzle platformer. Discover the journey of a sword-wielding warrior through dark and forgotten realms.

Explore a crumbling, nightmarish world, cut down enemies, evade deadly traps, and struggle to escape the evil that awaits...

Features

A hauntingly atomspheric pixel art world

Explore a broken nightmare world, defeat enemies, and avoid deadly traps to escape.

Play as three main characters

A knight explores a castle, a child uncovers secrets, and a wanderer seeks answers.

Roguelike Experience

Each level offers a different experience; no two levels are the same.

Escape an otherworldly labyrinth

Lure enemies into deadly traps, solve puzzles, and use your scythe, hookshot, and pickaxe to survive.
